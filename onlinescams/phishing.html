About

Phishing is a type of cyber attack where scammers try to trick individuals into revealing sensitive information, such as usernames, passwords, credit card numbers, or other personal details. These attacks typically involve sending fraudulent emails, text messages, or messages through other communication channels that appear to be from reputable sources, such as banks. The ultimate goal of phishing is to steal valuable information for financial gain, identity theft, or other malicious purposes.Phishing attacks can be highly convincing, so staying vigilant and following these precautions is crucial. By being cautious and practicing good cybersecurity hygiene, you can significantly reduce your risk of falling victim to phishing scams.
